# Phase 1.4: SEO Baseline

- Objective: Assess current on-page and technical SEO readiness for OSIRIS-SITE and Arabic content.
- Date: 2026-03-31
- Status: in_progress

Scope
- Meta titles/descriptions, canonical URLs, hreflang, sitemap.xml presence.
- Open Graph / Twitter Card data, structured data readiness.
- Language attribute consistency (lang="ar", dir="rtl").

Baseline Assessments
- Review existing pages for title consistency and length.
- Check canonical links and hreflang annotations.
- Validate sitemap inclusion and robots.txt behavior.
- Validate structured data presence where applicable.

Remediation Suggestions (Phase-2)
- Optimize missing/duplicate meta tags; align with content strategy.
- Add/verify hreflang for Arabic content variants and ensure canonical URLs are correct.
- Implement and validate schema.org structured data where relevant.

Deliverables
- SEO Baseline report with issues and suggested fixes.
- Phase-2 backlog with SEO improvements.

End of Phase 1.4
