# Risk Management Framework

This document provides a framework for identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks throughout the web development lifecycle.

## Risk Management Process

1.  **Risk Identification**: Proactively identify potential risks at the beginning of the project and at the start of each phase.
2.  **Risk Assessment**: Assess the likelihood and impact of each identified risk.
3.  **Risk Mitigation**: Develop strategies to mitigate or eliminate each risk.
4.  **Risk Monitoring**: Continuously monitor risks and the effectiveness of mitigation strategies.

## Risk Assessment Matrix

Use the following matrix to assess the severity of each risk.

| Likelihood | Impact | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| High | High | Critical |
| High | Medium | High |
| High | Low | Medium |
| Medium | High | High |
| Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Medium | Low | Low |
| Low | High | Medium |
| Low | Medium | Low |
| Low | Low | Low |

## Common Risks and Mitigation Strategies

The following table lists common risks in web development projects and potential mitigation strategies.

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Scope Creep** | High | High | Implement a change control process and clearly define the project scope in the project specification. |
| **Budget Overruns** | Medium | High | Create a detailed budget and track expenses closely. |
| **Timeline Delays** | Medium | High | Create a realistic timeline and track progress closely. |
| **Technical Debt** | High | Medium | Implement code reviews and a technical debt management plan. |
| **Security Vulnerabilities** | Medium | High | Implement a security checklist and conduct regular security testing. |
| **Performance Issues** | Medium | High | Implement a performance optimization guide and conduct regular performance testing. |
| **Accessibility Issues** | Medium | Medium | Implement an accessibility compliance guide and conduct regular accessibility testing. |
| **Stakeholder Misalignment** | Medium | High | Implement a stakeholder communication plan and hold regular stakeholder meetings. |
